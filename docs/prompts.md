# AI Image Prompts for UI Screens

Use these prompts to generate concept/background artwork for each app screen. Keep a cohesive visual style across all images: **modern baseball franchise management UI, clean mobile-first composition, cinematic but readable lighting, realistic sports branding without using real MLB logos, subtle blue/slate palette with warm accent colors**.

- **Global style anchor (use with every prompt):**
  `High-quality digital illustration for a mobile baseball franchise simulator, portrait orientation (9:16), layered composition with clear foreground/midground/background, soft depth of field, polished game UI key-art style, no text, no logos, no watermarks.`

- **Home / Franchise Dashboard (`index`)**  
  `Team owner office overlooking a baseball stadium at dusk, desk with lineup cards, scouting reports, budget sheets, tablet showing team metrics, mood of strategic planning and ambition, cinematic warm desk light against cool stadium lights.`

- **Intro Story Screen (`intro`)**  
  `Emotional “club inheritance” moment inside an old baseball clubhouse, framed historic team photos, worn championship pennant, mentor figure handing over a binder to a new manager, hopeful yet high-pressure tone, dramatic rim lighting.`

- **Onboarding Walkthrough (new-user guidance cards)**  
  `Sequence-friendly onboarding art showing a new manager learning team controls: scouting board, roster magnets on a tactical board, finance ledger, and schedule calendar in one cohesive montage, inviting and educational atmosphere.`

- **Weekly Results (`results`)**  
  `Post-game recap environment with scoreboard glow, stat sheets pinned on a wall, game balls and bats nearby, subtle confetti and rain contrast to imply wins/losses over a week, analytical yet dramatic presentation.`

- **Roster Management (`roster`)**  
  `Locker room strategy scene with player jerseys, medical and conditioning notes, player profile cards spread across a planning table, coach silhouettes discussing lineup depth, realistic textures and organized chaos.`

- **Lineup (`lineup`)**  
  `Baseball field tactical diagram blended with player cards and batting order slots, illuminated chalkboard style strategy overlays, focus on positional balance and game-day decision making.`

- **Standings (`standings`)**  
  `League command center with large standings board, team markers moving up and down rankings, tense competitive atmosphere, cool overhead lighting with bright highlight on top positions.`

- **Schedule (`schedule`)**  
  `Wall-sized season calendar with highlighted game days, travel routes between stadium cities, ticket stubs and weather notes pinned around dates, sense of rhythm and long-season progression.`

- **Finances (`finances`)**  
  `Front-office finance room with revenue charts, payroll graphs, stadium upgrade blueprint, stacks of invoices and sponsorship documents, visual tension between growth and debt management.`

- **Promotion Tracker (`promotion`)**  
  `Dramatic progression ladder concept showing club climbing divisions, banners for milestones, performance indicators glowing as thresholds are reached, triumphant but uncertain mood.`

- **Inbox / Messages (`inbox`)**  
  `Manager communication hub with emails, memos, scout alerts, and press notes appearing as layered cards on a digital desk interface, urgent notifications mixed with routine updates.`

- **Season Summary (`summary`)**  
  `End-of-season reflection scene in a quiet stadium at sunset, trophy case, final standings printout, season stat dossier, bittersweet accomplishment and forward-looking tone.`

- **Save / Load (`saves`)**  
  `Archive room metaphor with illuminated file slots representing save slots, timeline markers, secure digital vault aesthetic, clean and dependable mood.`

## Optional prompt modifiers

- **Visual consistency:** `same art direction as previous image set, matching color grading and lighting language`
- **Higher realism:** `ultra-detailed materials, realistic stadium architecture, physically based lighting`
- **More stylized:** `semi-stylized sports management concept art, bold silhouettes, painterly texture`
- **Negative prompt:** `no text, no watermark, no brand logos, no distorted hands, no blurry faces, no low-resolution artifacts`
