# First 10 Minutes Gameplay Design
## Baseball Franchise Simulator

The **first 10 minutes of gameplay** are the most important part of the game experience. In mobile game design this is called the **FTUE (First Time User Experience)**.

If players feel confused, overwhelmed, or bored during this phase, most will quit and never return.

For the **inherit-your-grandfather’s baseball team simulator**, the first 10 minutes should accomplish three things:

1. Create **emotional attachment**
2. Give the player **immediate control**
3. Establish a **clear long-term goal (promotion)**

Below is a recommended minute-by-minute flow.

---

# Minute 0–1: Story Hook

Start with a short cinematic-style intro.

### Scene Concept
- Old baseball stadium
- Empty stands
- Dusty locker room
- Letter from grandfather

### Example Narration

> “Your grandfather built this team from nothing.  
> Now the future of the club is in your hands.”

Reveal the starting team:

```
TEAM: Norfolk Clippers
LEAGUE: Coastal Amateur League
STADIUM: Harbor Field
```

Player customization:

- Owner name
- Optional team nickname

This creates **immediate ownership and emotional investment**.

---

# Minute 1–3: Quick Win

Players should experience **success immediately**.

Instead of giving full control, guide the player through a simple action.

### Tutorial Prompt

```
Your roster needs a pitcher.
Let's scout a player.
```

### Example Prospect Choices

| Player | Rating | Cost |
|------|------|------|
| Jake Morales | ⭐⭐⭐ | $20k |
| Ryan Chen | ⭐⭐ | $10k |
| Luis Ortega | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | $35k |

Player signs one.

Immediately after:

```
Your first exhibition game is ready.
```

The player simulates or plays the game.

**Result:** First win.

Early victories provide a **dopamine reward** that encourages continued play.

---

# Minute 3–5: Introduce Progression

Reveal the **league promotion ladder**.

```
COASTAL AMATEUR LEAGUE
↓
REGIONAL LEAGUE
↓
NATIONAL LEAGUE
↓
PROFESSIONAL LEAGUE
```

Message shown to player:

> “Finish in the top 2 to earn promotion.”

Players now understand the **core objective of the game**.

---

# Minute 5–7: First Strategic Decision

Introduce a simple management mechanic.

### Training Focus

```
Choose your team's focus:

⚾ Power hitting  
⚾ Pitching development  
⚾ Defensive discipline
```

This gives players the feeling that their **decisions affect the team**.

---

# Minute 7–8: First Rewarded Ad Opportunity

Introduce the monetization system in a **non-intrusive way**.

Example prompt:

```
Your scout found a promising prospect.
Watch a short video to unlock the full scouting report.
```

Important rules:

- Ads are **optional**
- Ads give **clear rewards**
- Never force ads during the tutorial

This establishes the **rewarded ad economy**.

---

# Minute 8–9: Emotional Narrative Moment

Reinforce the story element.

Example message:

> “Your grandfather always believed this team could reach the majors.”

Show current record:

```
SEASON RECORD
2–0
```

This strengthens **player attachment to the team**.

---

# Minute 9–10: Long-Term Hook

End the tutorial with a clear goal.

```
NEXT GOAL
Win 8 more games to reach the playoffs
```

Reward preview:

```
Promotion Bonus
$500k
New Stadium Upgrades
```

Players now understand what they are working toward.

---

# What the Player Has Learned

After the first 10 minutes the player understands:

- How to scout players
- How games work
- The league promotion ladder
- The reward system
- The long-term objective

Most importantly:

```
The player feels like they own the team.
```

---

# Psychological Hooks Used

| Hook | Where it Appears |
|-----|-----|
| Story | Grandfather introduction |
| Success | First exhibition win |
| Ownership | Team customization |
| Progression | Promotion ladder |
| Rewards | Scouting bonus |
| Long-Term Goal | Promotion objective |

These hooks are essential for **player retention**.

---

# Key App Store Marketing Insight

Your **promotion ladder concept** should appear clearly in your App Store screenshots.

Example marketing line:

> “Start with a small-town team and climb the leagues to the pros.”

This communicates the entire game loop in a single sentence.

---

# Summary

The first session must deliver:

- Emotional connection
- Early success
- Clear progression
- A strong goal

When done correctly, players will naturally continue playing beyond the first session.

```
Ownership + Progression = Long-term engagement
```
